player multithread byte statuses:

1 - player rang in
2 - add player to early ring-in penalty table
3 - enabler is now active
4 - enabler is inactive
5 - clear penalty table
6 - P1 ring-in success
7 - P2 ring-in success
8 - P3 ring-in success
9 - (inside thread only) wait for enabler to go active and then activate penalty
